Extending Portolan with plug-ins

Every tool provider can enhance Portolan (or insert its tool into the Portolan application) by the mean of Eclipse plug-ins mechanism.

Such plug-ins can added behavior to Portolan by declaring appropriate extensions. In its current state of development, Portolan offers two extensions points which may be use to insert additional commands:

  • the builder extension point: fr.inria.portolan.common.builder.
  • the editor (also named 'Full View') extension point: fr.inria.portolan.visualization.fullView.
See their dedicated page for more informations about their use and effects.
